## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no login, no trial. The audit is free because it's a lead-generation tool for Credex — we're transparent about that.

**Q: How is the pricing data kept current?**
A: Every price in the audit engine is manually verified against official vendor pricing pages weekly. Sources are cited in our public PRICING_DATA.md file.

**Q: Will you email me without permission?**
A: No. Email is only requested after you've seen your audit results. You can view and share your results without giving us your email at all.

**Q: How does the AI summary work?**
A: After the rule-based audit runs, we send your results to an AI model which writes a 80-100 word personalized summary. The audit math is not AI — it's deterministic rules based on real pricing data.

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise — sourced from companies that overforecast. If your audit shows significant savings, Credex may be able to help you capture more of that saving through discounted credits.
