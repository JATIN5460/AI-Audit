# ECONOMICS.md

## What a converted lead is worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits. Average deal size estimated at $2,000-$5,000 in credit purchases per company per year, with 20-30% margin. Conservative estimate: $600 gross profit per converted customer. With 40% repeat purchase rate over 2 years, LTV ~ $840 per customer.

## CAC at each channel

| Channel | Est. CAC |
|---|---|
| Hacker News organic | $0 — time cost only |
| Cold DM on X | $0 — time cost only |
| Newsletter mention | $0-$200 (if paid placement) |
| Paid social (future) | $150-$300 per lead |

At $0 CAC for organic channels and $840 LTV, payback is immediate on organic. Even at $300 CAC paid, unit economics work.

## Conversion funnel math

- 1,000 audits completed/month
- 25% email capture rate = 250 leads/month
- 15% of leads have >$500/mo savings (Credex CTA shown) = 37 high-intent leads
- 20% book a consultation = 7-8 consultations/month
- 50% of consultations convert to credit purchase = 3-4 customers/month
- At $600 gross profit per customer = $1,800-$2,400 gross profit/month from the tool

## What makes this profitable

The tool is free to run. Supabase free tier handles 500MB — enough for ~50,000 audits. Vercel free tier covers hosting. Gemini API free tier covers summaries. Resend free tier covers 3,000 emails/month. Total infrastructure cost at early scale: $0/month.

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 months

Month 1-3: 300 audits/month, 3 customers/month, $1,800/month gross profit
Month 4-6: HN post drives spike to 2,000 audits/month, 12 customers/month, $7,200/month
Month 7-12: Newsletter distribution, 5,000 audits/month, 30 customers/month, $18,000/month
Month 13-18: Paid distribution kicks in, 15,000 audits/month, 90 customers/month, $54,000/month

$54,000/month gross profit × 12 = $648,000 ARR — close to $1M but needs either higher deal size (enterprise contracts) or referral loop (both parties get perk) to close the gap. Enterprise contracts at $5,000+ deal size get there faster.
